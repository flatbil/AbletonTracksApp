Installation Complete

StagePad Bridge is installed and running in the background.

Finish setup in Ableton Live:

  1. Open Ableton Live
  2. Go to Preferences → Link/Tempo/MIDI
  3. Under Control Surface, select AbletonOSC in any open slot
  4. Open StagePad on your iPad — it will connect automatically

To view bridge logs:
tail -f ~/Library/Logs/StagePadBridge/bridge.log

To uninstall, run Uninstall StagePad Bridge from the same folder you downloaded this installer.
