Welcome to MD Buddy Bridge

This installer sets up the bridge that connects Ableton Live on your Mac to the MD Buddy iPad app.

The installer will:

  • Install the bridge service (auto-starts at login, restarts if it crashes)
  • Install the AbletonOSC remote script into your Ableton User Library

After installation, open Ableton Live and go to
Preferences → Link/Tempo/MIDI → Control Surface
and select AbletonOSC in one of the slots.

Then open MD Buddy on your iPad — it will find the bridge automatically.
